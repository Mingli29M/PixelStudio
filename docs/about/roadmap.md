# Roadmap

PixelStudio's development roadmap and planned features.

## Project Vision

PixelStudio aims to be a comprehensive, open-source pixel art creation tool that rivals commercial alternatives while remaining free and accessible to all artists.

## Current Status

**Version:** Pre-Alpha / Early Development  
**Release:** Not yet available

The project is currently in the early stages of development. Core systems are being implemented and tested.

## Priorities

Our development priorities:

1. **Stability** - Reliable, bug-free experience
2. **Performance** - Fast and responsive
3. **Usability** - Intuitive and easy to use
4. **Features** - Comprehensive toolset
5. **Compatibility** - Cross-platform support
6. **Community** - Open and collaborative

## Long-term Vision

PixelStudio aspires to:

- Be the **go-to open-source pixel art tool**
- Support artists from beginners to professionals
- Foster a vibrant community of pixel artists
- Provide a free alternative to commercial tools
- Push the boundaries of pixel art creation
- Integrate with modern game development workflows
